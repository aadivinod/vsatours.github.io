Kashmir

Kashmir, in the northern part of the Indian subcontinent, is renowned for its stunning natural beauty and ongoing political conflicts. It's a region of breathtaking landscapes, but also long-standing territorial disputes between India, Pakistan, and China, making it a complex and sensitive area of international concern.

Dal Lake

Dal Lake is a picturesque, iconic lake in Srinagar, Kashmir, surrounded by lush greenery and houseboats. It's a major tourist attraction known for its serene beauty, Shikara rides, and floating gardens.

Amritsar

Amritsar, a vibrant city in the Indian state of Punjab, is renowned for its cultural richness and spiritual significance. It's home to the magnificent Golden Temple, a sacred Sikh shrine, and also known for its delicious Punjabi cuisine.

Wayanad

Wayanad, a stunning hill district in Kerala, India, is celebrated for its lush green landscapes, abundant wildlife, and serene tea and coffee plantations. It's a nature lover's paradise and a popular destination for trekking and ecotourism.
